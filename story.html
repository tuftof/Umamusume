Story Timeline

Chapter 1: Set Off for the Brightest Star

It follows the journey of Mejiro McQueen as she takes on the role of team ace for Team Sirius, overcoming the pressure of leadership and the team's near-disbandment after the departure of key members, including Oguri Cap and her previous trainer. .

Chapter 2: Little Big Ambition

It focuses on Rice Shower as she confronts her insecurities and grows into a more confident racer.The story unfolds through four races, with the player helping her overcome self-doubt, culminating in her receiving the song "A Little Prayer" and the SSR support card "Rice Shower (Showered in Joy)".

Chapter 3: Embarking on the Dream Express

It centers on Winning Ticket as she strives to win the Japanese Derby and prove herself against her rivals.The chapter features four medium-distance races and rewards the player with the song "Those Tears Are for Tomorrow's Sake!" and the SSR support card "Winning Ticket (Dreams Do Come True!)".

Chapter 4: Roiling Craving

It is dedicated to Narita Brian, exploring her ambitions and the complex relationship with her older sister, Biwa Hayahide.The chapter includes a mix of race distances—mile, medium, and long—requiring strategic use of different trainees, and concludes with the release of the concert "BLAZE," featuring Narita Brian as the main singer, along with the SSR support card "Narita Brian (Two Pieces)".
